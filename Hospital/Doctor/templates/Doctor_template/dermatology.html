Medecine and Health

The Department of Dermatology at Aster MIMS offers complete dermatologic care, providing treatments for general/clinical conditions such as disorders of the skin, hair, nail and venereal diseases.

Besides, the department also facilitates the treatment of cosmetic conditions such as acne, scars, pigmentation, vitiligo, hair loss, unwanted hair, moles, skin tags, warts, corns, wrinkles and so on

Having doctors with immense expertise and cutting-edge technical assistance make the department one of the best facilities in the hospital.
