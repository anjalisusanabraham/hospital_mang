Medecine and Health

The Heaven Psychiatry Department imparts comprehensive and compassionate patient care while dealing with child, adolescent, adult, and geriatric mental health.

The department of psychiatry and behavioural medicine provides psychological and pharmacological management for individuals suffering from psychiatric illnesses. The department of Psychiatry at BMH is committed to provide relief to patients suffering from psychiatric illnesses. Treatment is provided for a wide range of psychiatric disorders including schizophrenia, delusional disorder, panic disorder and OCD. Treatment is also available for sleeping, eating and sexual disorders. Counselling services are also provided. The experts at the department are highly qualified and experienced.
