Medecine and Health

Heaven Institute of Gastro Intestinal Sciences is the comprehensive centre for management of Gastrointestinal and liver disorders. The division works for proper understanding, prevention, diagnosis and treatment of diseases of digestive tract and liver through the integration of novel models of healthcare delivery with innovative programs in clinical and basic research.

The unit is a well renowned referral centre for advanced liver diseases and transplant hepatology. The centre is equipped with the best endoscopic suite offering complete endoscopic services including diagnostic and therapeutic endoscopic ultrasound (EUS), Double balloon enteroscopies, Esophageal and rectal manometry, Third space endoscopy, Wireless Capsule endoscopies and ERCP. The Paediatric GI and Hepatology services cover the whole spectrum of diagnostic and therapeutic liver care services.

The Integrated Liver Care service under the Division of Gastrointestinal Sciences at Heaven, Calicut is the sole comprehensive liver care unit of North Kerala. The centre has performed more than 150 liver transplants over a short span of time and is the single high volume liver transplant centre of North Kerala, with results at par with international standards. More than 70 liver transplants including six deceased donor transplants happened here during the Covid pandemic. Heaven Calicut is also one among the few centres of South India doing paediatric liver transplants and has recently performed a successful liver transplant for a baby weighing just 5.4Kg
